Banana Pancake Recipe

A simple and delicious breakfast option made with ripe bananas and basic pantry ingredients. These pancakes are naturally sweet and soft, perfect for a quick morning meal.

Preparation time
  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour/li>
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup milk (or dairy-free alternative)
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Mash the banana: In a bowl, mash the ripe banana until smooth.
  2. Mix wet ingredients Add the egg, milk, and vanilla extract (if using). Stir until combined.
  3. Combine dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
  4. Make the batter: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined. Avoid overmixing.
  5. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a little butter or oil.
  6. Cook the pancakes: Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan, forming pancakes. Cook for 2-3 minutes until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for another 2 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Serve and enjoy: Remove from heat and serve warm with your favorite toppings, such as syrup, fruit, or yogurt.
